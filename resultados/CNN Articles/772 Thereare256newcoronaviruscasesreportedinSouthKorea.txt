Article Title: There are 256 new coronavirus cases reported in South Korea, Posted On: 02-27-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-27-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 Chung Sung-Jun/Getty ImagesSouth Korea reported 256 more confirmed cases of the novel coronavirus on Friday morning, bringing the national total to 2,022, according to the South Korean Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (KCDC). The KCDC did not report any new deaths, meaning that the national death toll remains at 13. Among the 256 new cases, 182 are from Daegu, the epicenter of the outbreak in South Korea. A total of 1,314 cases since the beginning of the outbreak have come from Daegu, according to the KCDC. An additional 49 cases are from North Gyeongsang Province, which surrounds Daegu city.
