Article Title: Mexico reports fourth coronavirus case, Posted On: 02-29-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-29-20-intl-hnk/index.html
A 20-year-old who recently traveled to Italy has tested positive for the novel coronavirus in Mexico -- bringing the national total to four cases, said Coahuila Governor Miguel Ángel Riquelme on Saturday. The Mexican foreign ministry added that it had not received official word from Washington regarding possible transit restrictions over the US-Mexico border.“The Secretary of Health implements mitigation measures, in accordance with those recommended by WHO and similar to those of countries such as Canada and the United Kingdom, staying alert at points of entry into the country, in order to inform and assist people who manifest symptoms related to COVID-19 and come from countries with local community transmission of this new disease," the ministry said.
