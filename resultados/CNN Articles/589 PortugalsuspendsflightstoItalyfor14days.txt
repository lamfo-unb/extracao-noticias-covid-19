Article Title: Portugal suspends flights to Italy for 14 days, Posted On: 03-10-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-10-20-intl-hnk/index.html
Portugal is suspending all flights to Italy for a period of 14 days, ANAC, Portugal’s Civil Aviation Regulator, said in a statement on Tuesday.The order will become effective on March 11, ANAC said.
