Article Title: BREAKING: California governor orders all 40 million residents to stay at home to limit outbreak, Posted On: 03-19-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-19-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 Josh Edelson/AFP/Getty ImagesAll 40 million residents in the state of California have been ordered to stay home to prevent the spread of coronavirus. The state is one of the worst affected in the US after Washington and New York. There are now 910 coronavirus cases in California, including 19 deaths.
