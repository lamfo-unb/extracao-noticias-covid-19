Article Title: South Korean flights to repatriate citizens from Wuhan have been delayed, Posted On: 01-29-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-01-29-20-intl-hnk/index.html
The South Korean Foreign Affairs Ministry announced early Thursday morning (local time) that two planned charter flights to evacuate citizens from Wuhan had been delayed. The flights had been scheduled to depart from Wuhan at 3 p.m. and 5 p.m. local time. Now, the South Korean government is working with the Chinese government to organize flights as early as tonight, according to a Korean official.A planned flight to bring British citizens back from Wuhan today has also been delayed.
