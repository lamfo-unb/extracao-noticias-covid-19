Article Title: France orders 1 billion face masks from China, Posted On: 03-28-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-28-20-intl-hnk/index.html
France has ordered 1 billion face masks from China, French Health Minister Olivier Véran confirmed in a press conference on Saturday.Speaking alongside Prime Minister Édouard Philippe, Véran said the country could produce 8 million face masks a week but consumed 40 million each week.A week ago: Olivier Véran said France had ordered over 250 million masks amid the coronavirus outbreak in the country.
