Article Title: Kirkland puts more firefighters in quarantine after dealing with coronavirus patients, Posted On: 03-02-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-02-20-intl-hnk/index.html
The city of Kirkland, Washington, is putting two more firefighters in quarantine as a result of possible exposure to coronavirus. There are now 27 firefighters in quarantine after having responded to coronavirus calls at the Life Care Center nursing facility, city spokeswoman Kellie Stickney said in a statement That figure includes 19 firefighters who were moved into isolation, with some showing possible symptoms of coronavirus, according to Doug Stern, spokesman for the International Association of Fire Fighters.An additional 10 firefighters are in quarantine from the nearby Redmond and Woodinville Fire Departments, along with two Kirkland police officers.
