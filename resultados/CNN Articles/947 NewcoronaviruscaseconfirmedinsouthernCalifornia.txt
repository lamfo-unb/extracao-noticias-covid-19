Article Title: New coronavirus case confirmed in southern California, Posted On: 02-12-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-12-20-intl-hnk/index.html
A plane carrying evacuees from the virus zone in China lands at Marine Corps Air Station Miramar Wednesday, February 5.  Gregory Bull/AP/FILEA new case of the coronavirus has been confirmed in Southern California’s Marine Corps Air Station Miramar in San Diego County, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention press officer Ana Toro told CNN. This is the second evacuee from Wuhan, China under quarantine at MCAS Miramar to test positive. More details: This is the 14th confirmed case in the US and the eighth in California.
