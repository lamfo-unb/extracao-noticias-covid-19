Article Title: Washington state orders residents to stay at home for next 2 weeks, Posted On: 03-23-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-23-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 Elaine Thompson/APWashington state Gov. Jay Inslee issued a new order Monday night that requires residents to stay at home for the next two weeks.The order takes effect immediately. Exceptions were made for jobs deemed critical and grocery shopping."This weapon — distancing ourselves — is the only weapon we have against this virus," Inslee said in a televised address.
