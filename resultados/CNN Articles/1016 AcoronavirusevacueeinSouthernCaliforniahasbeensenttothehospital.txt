Article Title: A coronavirus evacuee in Southern California has been sent to the hospital, Posted On: 02-06-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-06-20-intl-hnk/index.html
An evacuee from Wuhan, China who arrived at Marine Corps Air Station Miramar in Southern California was transported to a local hospital after developing a fever or a cough, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) said in a statement Thursday.The adult was sent to UC San Diego Health, according to the statement.So far, a total of five evacuees from the base have been transported for further evaluation.
