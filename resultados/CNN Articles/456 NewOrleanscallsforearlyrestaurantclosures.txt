Article Title: New Orleans calls for early restaurant closures, Posted On: 03-15-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-2-03-15-20-intl-hnk/index.html
People wait in line outside the renowned Cafe Du Monde in the French Quarter of New Orleans on Sunday. Chris Graythen/Getty ImagesNew Orleans Mayor LaToya Cantrell has ordered all full-service restaurants with seating capacity to close at 9 p.m., according to a release from the city. Mayor Cantrell has further asked establishments to attempt to reduce capacity by half.
