Article Title: 2020 Fed Cup qualifier moved from China amid coronavirus fears, Posted On: 01-26-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-hnk-intl-01-26-20/index.html
The International Tennis Federation announced on Sunday that February’s regional Fed Cup qualifying event will be moved from Dongguan, China, to Nur-Sultan, Kazakhstan."The decision to move the event to a different venue was taken due to increasing travel restrictions in China, P.R. at the present time and followed consultation with independent security advisors,” the ITF said in a statement.The event will take place February 4 - 8. The top two teams advance to the Fed Cup playoffs in April.
