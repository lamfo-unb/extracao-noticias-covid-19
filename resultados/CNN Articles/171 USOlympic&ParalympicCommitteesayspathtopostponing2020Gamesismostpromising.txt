Article Title: US Olympic & Paralympic Committee says path to postponing 2020 Games is "most promising", Posted On: 03-23-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-23-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 Jae C. Hong/APIn a statement released Monday night, United States Olympic & Paralympic Committee CEO Sarah Hirshland and Chair Susanne Lyons said that the organization has heard the concerns from Team USA athletes regarding the upcoming 2020 Games, and that the “path toward postponement is the most promising.” Hirshland and Lyons continued: “We encourage the IOC to take all needed steps to ensure the Games can be conducted under safe and fair conditions for all competitors.”
