Article Title: Five Wuhan coronavirus cases now confirmed in the US, Posted On: 01-26-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-hnk-intl-01-26-20/index.html
Five cases of Wuhan coronavirus have been confirmed in the United States, the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said on Sunday.One new case was confirmed in Arizona and two cases were confirmed in California. Cases had previously been confirmed in Illinois and Washington state. All the cases were in people who had recently traveled to Wuhan, China.
