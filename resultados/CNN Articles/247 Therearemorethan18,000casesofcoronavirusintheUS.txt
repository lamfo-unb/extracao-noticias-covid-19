Article Title: There are more than 18,000 cases of coronavirus in the US, Posted On: 03-20-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-20-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 David J. Phillip/APThere are 18,170 confirmed coronavirus cases in the United States, and 241 people have died of the virus, according to CNN Health’s tally of US cases that are detected and tested in the United States through US public health systems. The total includes cases from all 50 states, the District of Columbia and other US territories, as well as all repatriated cases.
