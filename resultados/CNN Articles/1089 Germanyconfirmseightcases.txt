Article Title: Germany confirms eight cases, Posted On: 02-01-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-01-20-intl-hnk/index.html
The eighth confirmed case of the novel coronavirus in Germany is a 33-year-old man hospitalized in the state of Bavaria, health officials said Saturday. The patient is the seventh employee of a company in the district of Starnberg who has been diagnosed, the Bavarian Health Ministry said.The case was confirmed after all employees at the company were tested in the past three days.
