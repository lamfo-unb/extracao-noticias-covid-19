Article Title: Brazil announces second case of coronavirus, Posted On: 02-29-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-29-20-intl-hnk/index.html
Brazil’s health ministry announced the country’s second confirmed case of the novel coronavirus on Saturday.The patient had been in Italy and traveled from Milan to Sao Paulo on Feb. 27, according to the ministry, which added that “there are no elements that indicate that the virus has circulated in the country.”
