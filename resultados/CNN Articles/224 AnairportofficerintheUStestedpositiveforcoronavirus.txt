Article Title: An airport officer in the US tested positive for coronavirus , Posted On: 03-21-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-21-20-intl-hnk/index.html
A Transportation Security Administration officer working at Dulles International Airport in Virginia has tested positive for the coronavirus.The individual has been quarantined and is resting at home, the TSA said in a statement.Here's what else the statement said:“TSA confirms that a Transportation Security Administration officer who works at Washington Dulles International Airport tested positive for the Covid-19 virus.The individual is quarantined and resting at home. TSA employees who work the same shift and may have come in contact with the officer who tested positive during the past 14 days have been alerted about the situation so that they can take action as appropriate."
