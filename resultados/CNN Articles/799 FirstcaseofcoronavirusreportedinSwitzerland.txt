Article Title: First case of coronavirus reported in Switzerland, Posted On: 02-25-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-25-20-hnk-intl/index.html
A 70-year-old man is the first person to test positive for coronavirus in Switzerland, the country’s Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH) told CNN. The Swiss man from the canton of Ticino, a region which borders Italy, had been to Lombardy, Italy, in mid-February.He has been hospitalized, where he's “under isolation and in good health so far," a FOPH spokesperson said.Italy is the site of Europe's biggest coronavirus outbreak. The cases there are heavily concentrated in the region of Lombardy.
