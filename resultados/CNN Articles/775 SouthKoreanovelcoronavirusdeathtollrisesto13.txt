Article Title: South Korea novel coronavirus death toll rises to 13, Posted On: 02-27-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-27-20-intl-hnk/index.html
South Korea has reported one new death from novel coronavirus in the city of Daegu, bringing the total number of coronavirus deaths in the country to 13, Daegu’s mayor Kwon Young-jin said in a press briefing on Thursday.The victim was a 74-year-old man and a member of Shincheonji religious group who died on Thursday morning, Kwon added.South Korea has the most confirmed coronavirus cases outside of mainland China.
