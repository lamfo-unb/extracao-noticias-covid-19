Article Title: Maine governor declares state of emergency, recommends school closures, Posted On: 03-15-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-2-03-15-20-intl-hnk/index.html
Maine Gov. Janet Mills speaks at a news conference in Augusta, Maine, on Thursday.  Robert F. Bukaty/APMaine Gov. Janet Mills declared a state of emergency Sunday and recommended school closures statewide, according to a news release from her office.Mills is recommended postponing events with 50 or more people as well as the postponement of "all non-urgent medical procedures, elective surgeries, and appointments at hospitals and health care providers across the state until further notice."
