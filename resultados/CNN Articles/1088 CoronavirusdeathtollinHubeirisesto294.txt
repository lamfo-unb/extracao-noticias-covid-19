Article Title: Coronavirus death toll in Hubei rises to 294, Posted On: 02-01-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-01-20-intl-hnk/index.html
People line up outside a fever clinic at Wuhan Union Hospital on January 31. Chinatopix/APForty-five more people have died in China's Hubei province, bringing the total death toll in Hubei to 294, health officials said. Nearly 2,000 new cases of coronavirus were confirmed Saturday in the province, which is the epicenter of the outbreak. These updated figures push the total number of confirmed cases in mainland China to more than 13,700.
