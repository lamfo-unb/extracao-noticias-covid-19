Article Title: President Trump says he is considering an executive order freeing elderly nonviolent federal prisoners, Posted On: 03-22-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-22-20/index.html
President Donald Trump said Sunday he is considering an executive order to free elderly "totally nonviolent" federal prisoners."We have been asked about that, and we're going to take a look at it. It's a bit of a problem. But when we talk about totally nonviolent, we're talking about these are totally nonviolent prisoners, we're actually looking at that, yes," Trump told reporters at a White House news conference.Several states, including California, New York, Ohio and Texas, and New York City have started releasing low-level offenders as well as those who are elderly or sick because of the virus.
