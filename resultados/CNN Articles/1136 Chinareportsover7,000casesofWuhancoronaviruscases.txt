Article Title: China reports over 7,000 cases of Wuhan coronavirus cases, Posted On: 01-29-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-01-29-20-intl-hnk/index.html
At least 7,711 cases of Wuhan coronavirus have been confirmed in China, including 170 deaths as of the end of Wednesday, according to China’s National Health Commission. This number reflects an increase of 1,737 cases and 38 deaths from Tuesday.Tibet has now confirmed its first case of the coronavirus. All 31 provinces in mainland China have now reported cases of the Wuhan coronavirus.
