Article Title: Beijing announces 14-day quarantine requirement for returning residents, Posted On: 02-14-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-14-20-intl-hnk/index.html
All people returning to Beijing will be required to stay at home or under observation for 14 days in order to prevent the spread of the coronavirus, according to China’s state-run news agency Xinhua.The Leading Group of Beijing New Coronavirus Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention and Control Office, which made the announcement Thursday night (local time), said that any individual refusing to do so will be “held accountable according to law.”
