Article Title: Three more cases of Wuhan coronavirus confirmed in Singapore, Posted On: 01-29-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-01-29-20-intl-hnk/index.html
Singapore’s Ministry of Health has confirmed three more cases of Wuhan coronavirus Thursday morning, bringing the country’s total to 10. All three cases are Chinese nationals who traveled from Wuhan, according to a statement from Singapore’s Health Ministry. Among the new cases are a married couple — a 56-year-old man and 56-year-old woman— and another 56-year-old man, who are in isolation rooms at the National Center of Infectious Diseases. All three new patients were asymptomatic during the flight to Singapore.
