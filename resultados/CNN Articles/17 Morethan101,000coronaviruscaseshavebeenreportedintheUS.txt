Article Title: More than 101,000 coronavirus cases have been reported in the US, Posted On: 03-27-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-27-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 John Minchillo/APThere are at least 101,242 cases of the novel coronavirus in the United States, according to CNN Health’s tally of US cases that are detected and tested in the United States through US public health systems.At least 1,588 people have died in the US from coronavirus. Today there have been at least 402 new deaths reported. That is the most deaths reported in the US in a single day. The previous single day high of deaths reported in the US came yesterday. There were 253 deaths reported yesterday.The total includes cases from all 50 states, Washington, D.C., and other US territories, as well as all repatriated cases.
