Article Title: All 11 US coronavirus patients are "doing well," CDC official says, Posted On: 02-05-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-05-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 Jessica McGowan/Getty ImagesAll 11 patients diagnosed with the coronavirus in the United States are “doing well,” according to an official with the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.“The 11 US patients, as far as I know, are all doing well,” said Dr. Nancy Messonnier, director of the CDC's National Center for Immunization and Respiratory Diseases. "The ones who were sicker have improved.”The CDC plans to continue to monitor each patient's health.
