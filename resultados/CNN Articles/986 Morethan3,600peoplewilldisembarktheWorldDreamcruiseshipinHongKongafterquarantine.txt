Article Title: More than 3,600 people will disembark the World Dream cruise ship in Hong Kong after quarantine, Posted On: 02-09-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-09-20-intl-hnk/index.html
More than 3,600 people, which includes crew and passengers, can leave the World Dream cruise ship since being quarantined on February 5 after the health authorities announced that everyone on board had tested negative for coronavirus.The ship has been docked in Hong Kong.
