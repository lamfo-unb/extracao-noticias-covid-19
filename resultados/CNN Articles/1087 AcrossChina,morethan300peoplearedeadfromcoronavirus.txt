Article Title: Across China, more than 300 people are dead from coronavirus, Posted On: 02-01-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-01-20-intl-hnk/index.html
A total of 304 people had died from coronavirus in mainland China by the end of Saturday, according to the country's National Health Commission. So far, there have been no deaths outside the mainland.The vast majority of the deaths have occurred in the province of Hubei, of which Wuhan is the capital. Wuhan -- home to 11 million people -- is the epicenter of the outbreak. The total number of confirmed cases in mainland China is now 14,380 -- an increase of 2,590 from Friday's total.
