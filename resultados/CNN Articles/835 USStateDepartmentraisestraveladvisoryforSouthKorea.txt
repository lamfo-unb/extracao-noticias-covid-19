Article Title: US State Department raises travel advisory for South Korea, Posted On: 02-22-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-22-20-intl-hnk/index.html
The US State Department raised the travel alert for Americans to Level 2 on Saturday for travel to South Korea due to the coronavirus outbreak, according to a release from the department.The alert advises travelers to “exercise increased caution” and follow Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s guidelines in preventing illness such as avoiding sick people and frequent hand washing.
