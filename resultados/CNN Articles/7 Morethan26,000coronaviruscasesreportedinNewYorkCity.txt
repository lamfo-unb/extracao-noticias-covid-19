Article Title: More than 26,000 coronavirus cases reported in New York City, Posted On: 03-28-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-28-20-intl-hnk/index.html
New York City now has 26,697 cases of coronavirus, according to a press release from  Mayor Bill de Blasio’s office Friday.With the additional New York City cases, New York State now has 45,934 total cases.
