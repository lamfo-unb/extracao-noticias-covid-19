Article Title: A coronavirus patient is being transferred to Nebraska, Posted On: 02-24-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-24-20-hnk-intl/index.html
A man who tested positive for the novel coronavirus and was evacuated from the Diamond Princess cruise ship is being transferred to the University of Nebraska Medical Center from Lackland Air Force Base in Texas, according to a hospital statement.“This individual is the spouse of one of the 13 people who arrived in Omaha for monitoring and treatment February 17. This person will be taken directly to the National Quarantine Unit on the UNMC/Nebraska Medical Center campus,” the statement said.The hospital is monitoring 14 people, of which 12 have tested positive for coronavirus.
