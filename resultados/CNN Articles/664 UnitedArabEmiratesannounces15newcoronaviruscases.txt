Article Title: United Arab Emirates announces 15 new coronavirus cases, Posted On: 03-06-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-06-20-intl-hnk/index.html
The United Arab Emirates' Ministry of Health announced 15 new coronavirus cases on its official Twitter account Friday.So far seven people in the UAE have "recovered," including two Chinese nationals, according to the health ministry.
