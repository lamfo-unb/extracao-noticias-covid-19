Article Title: Kentucky confirms 1st coronavirus case, Posted On: 03-06-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-06-20-intl-hnk/index.html
Kentucky’s governor has confirmed the state’s first case of coronavirus this afternoon.Gov. Andy Beshear identified the person is in Lexington and they are being treated in isolation. Beshear has filed a state of emergency and activated the state’s emergency operations center.
