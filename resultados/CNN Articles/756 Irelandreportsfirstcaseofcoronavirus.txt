Article Title: Ireland reports first case of coronavirus, Posted On: 02-29-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-29-20-intl-hnk/index.html
A man in the eastern part of Ireland has contracted coronavirus, according to a statement from Ireland's National Public Health Emergency Team, Saturday.The case is associated with travel from an affected area in Northern Italy.
