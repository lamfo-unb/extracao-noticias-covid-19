Article Title: A ninth person has been diagnosed with coronavirus in the UK, Posted On: 02-12-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-02-12-20-intl-hnk/index.html
A ninth patient has been diagnosed with the novel coronavirus in the United Kingdom, Public Health England said in a statement on Wednesday.“One further patient in England has tested positive for novel coronavirus, bringing the total number of cases in the UK to nine,” the country's Chief Medical Officer Dr. Chris Whitty said in the statement.Whitty said the virus was “passed on in China" and that the patient was taken to a hospital in London.
