Article Title: Los Angeles will stop issuing parking tickets for next two weeks, mayor says, Posted On: 03-16-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-16-20-intl-hnk/index.html
 Robyn Beck/AFP/Getty Images/FileLos Angeles Mayor Eric Garcetti said the city will stop issuing parking tickets for the next two weeks effective immediately.Speaking at a news conference on Monday, Garcetti said there are more cars parked on city streets because people are working from home or are taking care of others.More on Los Angeles: On Sunday, Garcetti ordered the closure of bars, nightclubs, entertainment venues and gyms until March 31 in an effort to prevent the spread of coronavirus. His order also limits restaurants to takeout and delivery orders only.
