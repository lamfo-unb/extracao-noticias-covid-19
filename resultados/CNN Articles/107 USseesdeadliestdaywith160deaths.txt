Article Title: US sees deadliest day with 160 deaths, Posted On: 03-24-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-03-24-20-intl-hnk/index.html
There are at least 52,976 cases of the novel coronavirus in the United States and 704 people have died, according to CNN Health’s tally of US cases that are detected and tested in the US through public health systems.There have already been 163 deaths reported today, according to a tally by CNN, making this the deadliest day in the US since the coronavirus pandemic began. The total includes cases from all 50 states, the District of Columbia and other U.S. territories, as well as all repatriated cases.This post was updated to reflect the most recent figures.
