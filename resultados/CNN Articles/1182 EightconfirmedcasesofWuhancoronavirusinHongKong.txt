Article Title: Eight confirmed cases of Wuhan coronavirus in Hong Kong , Posted On: 01-26-20, At: https://www.cnn.com/asia/live-news/coronavirus-outbreak-hnk-intl-01-26-20/index.html
There are now eight confirmed cases of Wuhan coronavirus in Hong Kong, according to a government press release.The health department said it was continuing its epidemiological investigations and relevant contact tracing of the eight confirmed cases.This brings the total number of cases outside mainland China to 50, in an outbreak that has infected over 2,000 people worldwide and killed 56 in China.
